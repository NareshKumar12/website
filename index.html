Tagline Here

Preceptors Educational Services Pvt Ltd is a crew of educational and industrial experts, a network of worldwide leaders, doctorates and compassionate mentors whose task is to advise and guide every student or a working professional struck in selecting their main career stream, whose mission is to build the bridge between the worldwide mentors to every student or working experts who are confused to reach their dream careers.

The company foresees“PRECEPTORS MEET” hosted by doctorates and most experienced professors of various streams to break the confusions of the students and give them an advanced vision of the real world and the power to choose their FUTURE.
